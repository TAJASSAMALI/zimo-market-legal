Privacy Policy for Zimo Market

Last Updated: February 2026

1. Information We Collect

To provide our marketplace services, we collect:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, and phone number when you create a profile.
  • User Content: Product descriptions, prices, and images uploaded to ads.
  • Communications: Messages and images sent through our in-app chat system.
  • Device Data: Device ID and push notification tokens to alert you of new messages.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to facilitate buying and selling, verify users, and improve the app experience. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

3. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your data as long as your account is active. You have the right to delete your account at any time. Upon deletion, all personal data, ads, and chats are permanently erased from our primary databases.

For more details on how to delete your data, visit our Account Deletion Page.
