Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Zimo Market. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy governs the privacy practices of our mobile application and explains how we collect, safeguard, and disclose information that results from your use of our service.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us, as well as information generated automatically during your use of the app.

a. Account and Profile Information

  • Full Name and contact details
  • Email address and Phone number
  • Profile images and avatars
  • Encrypted authentication credentials

b. Marketplace Content

  • Product listings, descriptions, and item details
  • High-resolution images uploaded for advertisements
  • Pricing data and user-defined categories

c. Communications

  • Encrypted text messages sent via in-app chat
  • Media files and images shared within user conversations

d. Technical and Usage Data

  • Unique Device Identifiers (UUID)
  • Push notification tokens for real-time alerts
  • IP addresses and log data for security and fraud prevention

3. Use of Data

Zimo Market uses the collected data for various purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our marketplace functionality.
  • To facilitate secure communication between buyers and sellers.
  • To manage user accounts and provide customer support.
  • To monitor for fraudulent activity and ensure platform safety.
  • To deliver push notifications regarding ad status or messages.

Zimo Market does not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Data Security

The security of your data is a priority. All information collected by Zimo Market is encrypted in transit using industry-standard TLS/SSL protocols. Data is stored on secure servers with restricted access, utilizing modern encryption at rest to prevent unauthorized disclosure.

5. Account Deletion and Data Removal

We believe in user autonomy over their data. You may request the permanent deletion of your account and all associated data at any time through the app settings or our web portal.

Upon a valid deletion request:

  • Your personal profile and contact information are purged.
  • All active ads and marketplace listings are permanently removed.
  • Stored images (Profile, Ads, and Chats) are deleted from our cloud storage.
  • Chat histories are cleared.

To initiate this process, please visit our:
Account Deletion Request Page

6. Third-Party Services

We may employ third-party companies (such as Supabase for backend infrastructure) to facilitate our Service. These third parties have access to your data only to perform specific tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.
