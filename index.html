Zimo – Account Deletion

Zimo allows users to permanently delete their account and associated data.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the Zimo app
  2. Go to Profile → Settings
  3. Tap “Delete Account”
  4. Confirm deletion

Data Deleted

  • Profile information
  • Ads and listings
  • Messages and conversations
  • Favorites and follows
  • Brand registrations
  • Uploaded images

Data Retention

Backup logs may be retained for up to 30 days for security purposes. Some data may be retained if required by law.
